About Us

We (Tian Hui and Le Yi) started our study account in 2020, when we were secondary 2.

We were struggling to understand certain concept for our subjects such as math and science. Thus we turn to online learning material to help us understand better. However finding useful study material online was difficult. Therefore we decided to make an study account in hope that it will help others that are also struggling to understand certain concept for their subject.

We started off with an Instagram account, which we share study notes for some subject. Eventually, we started sharing tips and timelapse of us studying in hope to insipre others as well.

Along the way we have also work with other students to share study notes for other subjects.


Our Aim

Our objectives is to motivate students to strive for their personal best for their academic and personal life through the content that we post.
